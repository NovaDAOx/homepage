What is NOVA?

Nova is a tokenized solution for decentralized fundraising. Anyone can pledge $NOVA to their choice(s) from a library of projects curated by the governance DAO in return for a token share in the project. $NOVA is earned by staking our NFTs or bought on popular DEXs. Our aims are:

Democratize early fundraising while making it permisionless & borderless.

Harness the network effect of DAOs to create early stage growth flywheels.

Create a community of the most innovative minds in Web3 across art, economics and code.
