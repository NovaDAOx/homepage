NOVA DEUS

"I don't have much time... listen to me.... The Dark one is coming." said The Creator, struggling to speak.

The rain was deafening, the fog...blinding.

"That's impossible, we sealed him away, we gave our home to trap him inside the seventh gate!"

The apprentice said as he slammed his fist.

"It would seem we failed…find the Deus...or all hope is lost!" Said the Creator as the color of his eyes faded and so to did his life.

...just like that... the world as we knew it changed...

Forced to leave their celestial abode, the Deus now exist among us disguised as mere mortals. The Dark One has returned, and he's on a mission. Sealed by the Gods a millennia ago, only to escape and once again go for his final frontier…Earth. The Deus are the last line of defence, but stuck in a foreign world, they must adapt to the new conditions…and they must do it quickly. But before that, lies another problem. Where are they? They parted ways centuries ago and most haven’t seen each other since…some still live in the past. Now it all falls to one to reunite them all and lead them into battle to save their own creation, humanity. Join this legendary journey through time & space as one “man” is forced to call upon his past to save everyone’s future.
